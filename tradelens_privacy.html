Privacy Policy

App Name: TradeLens: Trading Log & Journal

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

TradeLens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by TradeLens.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Local Data Storage

TradeLens is designed as an offline-first application. The financial data you enter (including trade orders, positions, prices, and notes) is stored locally on your device using an internal database.

We do not transmit your personal trading data to external servers. Your financial logs remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to back them up or export them.

Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly within the app, specifically:

  • Trading transaction details (Entry/Exit price, quantity, symbol).
  • Strategy notes and journal entries.
  • Performance goals or settings.

2. Third-Party Services and APIs

Future Integrations

In future updates, TradeLens may offer the option to sync data from third-party brokerage services (e.g., Fyers, Zerodha) via their official APIs. This feature will be optional and user-initiated.

If you choose to use these features, TradeLens will only access "Read-Only" data required to generate your trading journal. We do not store your brokerage passwords; authentication is handled securely via the broker's login page.

Analytics

We may use standard analytical tools (like Google Firebase or Crashlytics) to collect anonymous, non-personal data about app stability and crash reports. This helps us fix bugs and improve the app. This data does not include your personal financial trade logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the TradeLens app.
  • Generate personal performance analytics (e.g., Hit Ratio, P&L Charts) for your viewing.
  • Fix technical issues and bugs.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial data to third parties.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, please be aware that no application or storage method is 100% secure. Since your data is stored locally, we recommend securing your device with a password or biometric lock to prevent unauthorized access to your journal.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy.
